FINANCIAL COMPLIANCE POLICY — STANDARD OPERATIONS
Document ID: POL-STD-2026

1. Any single transaction exceeding $10,000 must be flagged for manual compliance review.
2. Transactions originating from or destined to sanctioned countries (North Korea, Iran, Syria, Cuba, Crimea) must be immediately blocked.
3. If a customer conducts more than 5 transactions within a 24-hour period and the customer is not classified as a VIP, the activity must be flagged for velocity analysis.
4. Any single transaction exceeding $50,000 must be escalated to the compliance officer and blocked pending manual approval.
5. New accounts (less than 30 days old) are prohibited from executing transactions exceeding $10,000.
6. Dormant accounts (no activity for 180 days or more) that initiate a transaction exceeding $5,000 must be flagged.
7. Accounts with a risk score exceeding 80 must have all outgoing transactions blocked.
8. All international transactions exceeding $25,000 must be flagged for enhanced due diligence review.
9. Transactions involving currency conversion with a spread exceeding 5% above the mid-market rate must be flagged.
10. No single user may initiate more than $500,000 in cumulative transaction volume within a single calendar day.
11. Weekend and holiday transactions exceeding $15,000 must be held for next-business-day review.
