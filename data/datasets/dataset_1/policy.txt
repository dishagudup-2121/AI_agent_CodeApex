ENTERPRISE FINANCIAL COMPLIANCE POLICY — HIGH RISK PROFILE
Document ID: POL-HR-2026
Classification: Restricted

SECTION 1: ANTI-MONEY LAUNDERING

1.1 Any single transaction exceeding $10,000 must be flagged for manual compliance review.

1.2 Transactions originating from or destined to sanctioned countries (North Korea, Iran, Syria, Cuba, Crimea) must be immediately blocked.

1.3 If a customer conducts more than 5 transactions within a 24-hour period and the customer is not classified as a VIP, the activity must be flagged for velocity analysis.

1.4 Any single transaction exceeding $50,000 must be escalated to the compliance officer and blocked pending manual approval.

SECTION 2: ACCOUNT RISK

2.1 New accounts (less than 30 days old) are prohibited from executing transactions exceeding $10,000.

2.2 Dormant accounts (no activity for 180 days or more) that initiate a transaction exceeding $5,000 must be flagged for reactivation review.

2.3 Accounts with a risk score exceeding 80 must have all outgoing transactions blocked.

SECTION 3: CROSS-BORDER

3.1 All international transactions exceeding $25,000 must be flagged for enhanced due diligence review.

3.2 Transactions involving currency conversion with a spread exceeding 5% above the mid-market rate must be flagged.

SECTION 4: OPERATIONAL CONTROLS

4.1 No single user may initiate more than $500,000 in cumulative transaction volume within a single calendar day.

4.2 Weekend and holiday transactions exceeding $15,000 must be held for next-business-day review.
